ABOUT ME

With a background in Advertising and Marketing Management and a strong passion for technology, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical skill to front-end development. I specialize in crafting user-friendly experiences.

In addition to my marketing background, I also have a foundation in graphic design something I’ve been passionate about from an early age. This creative drive continues to shape the way I approach digital interfaces, from color theory and layout to visual storytelling and branding consistency.

After making the shift into tech through dedicated learning and hands-on projects, I’m now focused on growing as a junior developer, always curious and always improving.

My approach to development is deeply rooted in user experience. With a background in design thinking and communication from my studies, I build interfaces that are not only visually engaging but also intuitive and functional. Whether I’m working with React components or fine-tuning a layout in Figma, I aim to make every interaction feel smooth and purposeful.

I also enjoy bridging the gap between design and development, ensuring that the transition from concept to code is seamless. Clean, maintainable code and accessible interfaces are at the core of everything I create.

I'm committed to continuous learning and thrive in environments where I can contribute, collaborate, and grow. I believe that great digital products come from curiosity, empathy, and iteration and that mindset drives the way I write code, solve problems, and approach new challenges as I build my career in tech.
